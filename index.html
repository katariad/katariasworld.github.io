“User Experience Design (UXD or UED) is a design process whose sole objective is to design a system that offers a great experience to its users. Thus UXD embraces the theories of a number of disciplines such as user interface design, usability, accessibility, information architecture, and Human Computer Interaction..

Before starting to talk about the process of UX expert hiring, let’s, firstly, figure out who UX experts are and what are their primary responsibilities. The user experience specialist ensures that the web product and the presence of a set of online services are optimized for user needs, ensuring they are useful, attractive and easy to use. UX experts are in charge of all user experience activities for this group of services, collaborating closely with service management, development and delivery teams, and content, design, and development teams to create information architectures, user interface designs, and content strategies. They gain a deep understanding of user and customer needs by conducting primary research and collaborating with service teams and the sector intelligence team to gain access to additional research and information that helps paint a complete picture of web products and services, users and their needs.
